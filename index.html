ABOUT US

Our Quality is guaranteed by our Commitment to strive for Excellence.

Locani is based on the concept of family values as it is run by a husband and wife team. The name originated from our 3 daughters. This is why we are proud to say that our core objective is commitment – commitment to be successful and commitment to excel at satisfying our clients needs. Locani Instruments & Electrical is a Supplier of Pneumatics , Projects , Installations , Maintenance on instruments and electrical . The company’s main office is based in Westville and 646 Umbilo road .

MATHEW PILLAY


Locani is managed by Mathew Pillay : My passion for instruments and electrical stems from my experience gained from various aspects :

  • I qualified as an Instrument Mechanician in Jan 1990 (Trade Tested) – and went on to contracting for UIC – doing projects and loop checks and commissioning at Sappi Stanger and Mondi Merebank. I was given an opportunities to supervise a night shift crew of 21 people. (E&I)
  • After completion of the project I went on to work for Pat Marsh Agencies at Engen refinery for 5 weeks on a revamp at Combo. I started as a Tech and was promoted to supervisor of a small crew on commissioning
  • On completion I went to ISM Inter at Sasol Secunda to work on a shut where I did callibration of instruments , loop checks and modifications.
  • I went for military experience in 1993 / 4. Thereafter I took on a permanent job at Mondi Cartonboard Umgeni for 7 years. Due to the closing of the plant we relocated to Mondi Springs. After a year we returned to (Durban) Mondi Merebank and I realised shift work was not for me. This inspired me to venture into my own business.
  • To gain experience as an entrepreneur I worked for CTD at Engen refinery doing loop checks , calibrations ,QC and Loop packs, commissioning and maintenance for Group 5.
  • I was offered an opportunity to do projects , upgrades , maintenance at Nampak Tissue Riverview as MAP Instrumentation. I did a major upgrade on the E&I side. I did the complete installation (of a complete Pulper system) and commissioning. We also worked at Sappi Stanger doing the upgrade of Profibus System and also small projects and maintenance.
  • During a quiet period I was encouraged by Nampak to take a permanent position as process technician assisting to add more value to the company and plant’s.
  • I realised that I still wanted to be an entrepreneur and took the opportunity to work as a sales rep for Numatics SA in Durban gaining enormous experience on the products , sales and client rapport.
  • Thus LOCANI INSTRUMENTS &ELECTRICAL was started whereby it caters for instrument/electrical sales, services , maintenance and projects,preventative maintenance
