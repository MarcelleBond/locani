ABOUT US

Our Quality is guaranteed by our Commitment to strive for Excellence.

The brand Locani is an amalgamation of our 3 daughters names and directed by a husband and wife team. We are proud to state that our origins influence our core objective: commitment. 

We are a company formed on family values with a commitment to guarantee success in the satisfaction of our clients needs. 

Locani Instruments & Electrical expertise in the supply of pneumatic systems and equipment, the installation and maintenance on instruments as well as the execution electrical work required for each project.

MATHEW PILLAY

Locani is managed by Mathew Pillay.

Here he outlines his experiences and expertise.
"My passion for instruments and electrical stems from my experience gained from various aspects: ..."

  • I qualified as an Instrument Mechanician in Jan 1990 (Trade Tested) – and went on to contracting for UIC – executing projects and loop checks and commissioning at Sappi Stanger and Mondi Merebank. I was given an opportunity to supervise a night shift crew of 21 people. (E&I)
  • After completion of the project I went on to work for Pat Marsh Agencies at the Engen refinery for 5 weeks on a revamp in the Combo area. I started as a Technical engineer and was promoted to the supervisor of a small crew specialising in commissioning.
  • On completion I went to ISM Inter at Sasol Secunda to work on a shut* where I carried out instrument calibration, loop checks and modifications.
  • I went for military experience in 1993 until 1994. Thereafter I took on a permanent job at Mondi Cartonboard Umgeni for 7 years. Due to the closing of the plant we relocated to Mondi Springs. After a year we returned to (Durban) Mondi Merebank and I realised shift work was not for me. This inspired me to venture into my own business.
  • In order to gain experience as an entrepreneur, I worked for CTD at Engen refinery. My responsibilities included; performing loop checks , QC and loop packs, calibrations, commissioning and maintenance for Group 5.
  • I was offered an opportunity to do technical projects, upgrades, maintenance at Nampak Tissue Riverview as MAP Instrumentation. In that time, I did a major upgrade on the E&I side. I performed the complete installation of a complete Pulper system and as well as the commissioning. My team also worked at Sappi Stanger doing the upgrade of Profibus System in addition to small projects and maintenance.
  • During a quiet period I was requested by Nampak to take a permanent position as process technician, using my knowledge of the operations, repair and maintenance procedures to add value to the company and their plants.
  • I realised that I still wanted to be an entrepreneur and took the opportunity to work as a sales rep for Numatics SA in Durban gaining enormous experience on the products, sales and client rapport.
  • Thus LOCANI INSTRUMENTS & ELECTRICAL was founded. We focus and cater for instrument/electrical sales, services, maintenance and preventative maintenance projects.
